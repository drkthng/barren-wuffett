<objective>
Close Level 1 with the viral loop: a spoiler-free Level Complete screen, a one-tap Canvas share card, and rich link previews. After beating Mr. Market the player sees Barren's Wisdom quote and can share a 1200×630 quote card (no raw score) to their OS share sheet in a single tap; pasting the game URL in a messaging app renders a proper preview. This is the final MVP slice — the game now has a built-in growth mechanic and reads correctly when shared.

The critical correctness detail (RESEARCH Pitfall 4): the share Blob is generated and cached at BOSS_DEFEATED time, so the SHARE WISDOM tap calls navigator.share() directly in the user-gesture call stack — iOS Safari rejects share() if an await sits between the tap and the call.

Purpose: Deliver the "share my victory" capability and make the URL shareable, completing the Phase 2 success criteria.
Output: ShareService + ShareCard, Level Complete screen, og: meta tags + static og-image, and two Wave 0 tests.
</objective>

**As a** player who just beat the Level 1 boss, **I want to** share a spoiler-free wisdom card in one tap and have the game's URL preview nicely when I paste it, **so that** I can show friends without spoiling the game and they see an appealing preview.

<objective>
Add the two action set-pieces of Level 1: the paper-route timing mini-game and the Mr. Market boss fight. These build on the playable overworld from Plan 01 — the player now walks into trigger zones that hand off to dedicated scenes and hand back cleanly. The boss fight is where the value-investing lesson lands experientially: declining inflated Greed-phase offers builds a patience meter, and buying low in the Panic phase within the timer is the win — rushing to accept loses. This is the second MVP slice: after this plan a player can complete Level 1's mini-game and beat the boss, with patience materially rewarded (GAME-06).

Purpose: Deliver the "complete the level" capability on top of the "walk the level" capability.
Output: TriggerSystem + fully implemented PaperThrowScene + BossScene wired through the GameEvents bus and SaveService checkpoints.
</objective>

**As a** player, **I want to** play the paper-route mini-game and fight the Mr. Market boss where waiting and buying low beats rushing, **so that** I experience the value-investing lesson through gameplay and reach the end of Level 1.
